The Book of Sutures

By utilising scanned skull sutures as our training dataset, we developed application software capable of generating written representations that intentionally disregard any semantic relationships. The project generates text-like markings that, although displaying aesthetic qualities, remain devoid of linguistic meaning. The asemic product is therefore resistant to any historical precedent and retains no historically derived significance. Computationally produced sutures become political vectors that intimate at a future history of novel signifiers that effectively dematerialise the signified. This irrational surplus of unconscious production is an unfiltered communion from an impersonal creative impulse.

The book of sutures is a para-literary device and a new form of signification.
